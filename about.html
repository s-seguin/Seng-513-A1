STUART SEGUIN
computer science and the outdoors

Stuart is a Computer Science student at the University of Calgary with a strong passion for the outdoors. He strives to capture that passion through amateur photography. He loves mountain biking, skiing, hiking, back country camping and travelling. He recently returned from a four month trip to Australia and New Zealand.

What's in his bag?

Due to the nature of his activities, which is where Stuart takes most of his photos, they require smaller gear and a lighter bag as he has other gear to carry in case of emergency or if the weather gets bad.

Cameras
Sony A6000 Main camera for more serious photos.
iPhone 8 The camera I always have on me, good for quick snaps and travelling.
Lenses
SEL 18-135 My go to all around lense for the A6000.
Rokinon 12mm My ultrawide, great for landscapes and astro-photography.
