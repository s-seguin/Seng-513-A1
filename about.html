Stuart Seguin

Stuart is a Computer Science student at the University of Calgary with a strong passion for the outdoors. He strives to capture that passion through amateur photography.

He loves mountain biking, skiing, hiking, back country camping and travelling. He recently returned from a four month trip to Australia and New Zealand.
